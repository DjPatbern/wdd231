About CayPoint

CayPoint was born out of a vision to make healthcare access easier and more transparent. Whether you're new in town, seeking specialist care, or just trying to find the nearest gym, CayPoint is your reliable directory for verified and up-to-date health facilities.

We bridge the gap between individuals and health providers by using smart technology to help you locate health centers closest to you — hospitals, clinics, and fitness centers. Your health journey begins with finding the right support, and CayPoint makes that process seamless.
